About Me

I am currently seeking opportunities to leverage my technical expertise while further refining my skills to contribute to advancements in robotics, automation, and intelligent control systems. I recently completed MSc Robotics at School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Sheffield. As my dissertation project, I developed An Enhanced ORB-SLAM3 with Real-time Object Detection and Dynamic Object Handling, supervised by Dr Jonathan M. Aitken. Prior to this, I completed MEng Mechatronic Engineering at Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Nottingham Malaysia. As my dissertation project, I developed Brainwave-Controlled System for Smart Home Control, supervised by Dr Marwan Nafea.
